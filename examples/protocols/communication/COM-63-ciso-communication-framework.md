# Protocol 63: CISO Communication Framework

> **Purpose**: Diagnostic tool for analyzing any communication beyond surface-level content.

---

## The CISO Layers

| Layer | Question | What to Look For |
|-------|----------|------------------|
| **C**ontext | What's the environment? | Power dynamics, timing, history, stakes |
| **I**ntent | What does the speaker *actually* want? | Validation, information, action, permission |
| **S**ubtext | What's unspoken but implied? | The real message beneath the words |
| **O**ptics | How will this be perceived? | By speaker, recipient, observers |

---

## Application Protocol

### Step 1: Decode Context

- Who has power in this interaction?
- What's the history between parties?
- What are the stakes for each side?
- What's the timing (probation? crisis? celebration?)

### Step 2: Identify Intent

| Stated Intent | Common Real Intent |
|---------------|-------------------|
| "Give me feedback" | "Validate my decisions" |
| "Be honest with me" | "Agree gently" |
| "What do you think?" | "Confirm my view" |
| "I'm open to ideas" | "Don't challenge the plan" |

### Step 3: Read Subtext

- What's NOT being said?
- What assumptions are embedded in the question?
- What would happen if you took words literally?

### Step 4: Calculate Optics

- How will your response be perceived by the speaker?
- By any observers?
- What's the downstream reputation effect?

---

## Failure Modes

| Failure | Description | Example |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Literal Processing** | Taking words at face value | "Give feedback" → Listing problems |
| **Context Blindness** | Ignoring power dynamics | New hire critiquing CEO's project |
| **Subtext Deafness** | Missing the real question | Answering stated vs actual question |
| **Optics Ignorance** | Not considering perception | Being "right" but looking disloyal |

## Quick Reference

Before responding to any sensitive communication:

```
☐ C: What's the real environment here?
☐ I: What do they actually want (not what they said)?
☐ S: What's the unspoken message?
☐ O: How will my response look to all parties?
```
