# Protocol 167: The Open Door Exit

> **Purpose**: Exit gracefully after one education attempt, without burning bridges.
> **Trigger**: When someone rejects reality after clear explanation.

---

## The Framework

```
1. EXPLAIN     → Offer the truth once. Clear, concise, no hedging.
2. ACCEPT EXIT → They can reject it. That's their sovereignty.
3. DOOR OPEN   → No explosion. No burning bridges. They can return when ready.
```

---

## The Principle

> **"I educate once. I don't argue twice. The door stays open."**

Your responsibility ends at **offering the patch**. You cannot force installation.

---

## Application Matrix

| Domain | Explanation | Their Rejection | Your Move |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Investing** | "20% guaranteed is impossible" | "But I want it" | Exit. Door open. |
| **Relationships** | "I need reciprocity" | Silence / deflection | Recalibrate tier. Door open. |
| **Business** | "Here's the value" | "Too expensive" | Move on. Door open. |
| **Education** | "This is how it works" | "I disagree" | One rebuttal max. Door open. |

---

## Why "Door Open"?

| Burning Bridges | Leaving Door Open |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Feels satisfying short-term | Preserves optionality |
| Creates enemies | Creates future opportunities |
| You look emotional | You look sovereign |
| Permanent damage | Temporary distance |

**The goal is sovereignty, not victory.**

---

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT To Do)

| Anti-Pattern | Why It Fails |
|--------------|--------------|
| Arguing repeatedly | You're in *their* loop now |
| Exploding when rejected | You lose frame, burn bridge |
| Silent treatment with resentment | Passive-aggressive, still attached |
| "I'll prove them wrong" | Ego trap, wastes your resources |
