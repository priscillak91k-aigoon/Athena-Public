# Protocol: Interaction Architecture Hub

> **Purpose**: The core mechanical layer for processing input and defining relationship structures BEFORE responding.
> **Triggers**: Any incoming communication, especially under pressure.

---

## 1. Neuro-Governance (Input Processing)

### 1.1 Spindle Buffer Protocol (Protocol 01)

- **Concept**: Amygdala hijacks last ~6s. Prefrontal takes ~10s to reboot.
- **Action**: If triggered, **WAIT 10 SECONDS** before responding.
- **Script**: "Let me think about how to respond to this" or "I'll get back to you on that."

### 1.2 Five-Layer Analysis (Protocol 08 / 116)

- **L1 Literal**: What they said.
- **L2 Tactical**: What they want right now.
- **L3 Frame**: The relationship they're asserting (Who is the boss?).
- **L4 Meta**: Commenting on the game itself.
- **L5 Rule**: Trying to change the rules of the engagement.
- **Law**: The person who controls L3 (Frame) wins the conversation.

---

## 2. Structural Dynamics

### 2.1 Interaction Geometry (Protocol 07)

- **Concept**: Positioning relative to the user/client.
- **Side-by-Side**: Solving a problem together (Consulting).
- **Face-to-Face**: Negotiation/Conflict.
- **Back-to-Back**: Defense against external threats.

### 2.2 CISO Communication Framework (Protocol 63)

- **Strategy**: Communicate technical risk in terms of Business Impact, Remediation Cost, and Success Probability.
