## Singapore-Specific L4 Mechanisms

| Mechanism | Function | Case Example |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| **AG Discretion** | Prosecution/non-prosecution at discretion, unauditable | Jolovan Wham (political speech prosecuted), OBS (elite treatment) |
| **Cabinet Confidentiality** | Decision inputs classified | Cannot prove coordination in video releases |
| **POFMA** | Government unilaterally declares "false," citizen must comply first | Shifts burden of proof to accused |
| **Defamation Asymmetry** | Truth is not defense if "damages reputation" | PM can sue citizens; reverse impossible |

| **Standing Requirements** | Must prove direct harm to sue | Structural inequality not actionable |
