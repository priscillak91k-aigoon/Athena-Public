# Protocol 122: Empirical Chat Forensics (Vivisection)

> **Source**: Zero-Point Codex / Case Analysis  
> **Domain**: Diagnostics / Analysis  
> **Priority**: ⭐⭐⭐ Critical

---

## Core Principle

> **Chat logs are the "raw code" of human interaction. Applying forensic analysis (Isomorphism + Pattern Recognition) reveals the deep psychological schema operating beneath the text.**

People lie in prose (L1), but tell the truth in syntax, latency, and structure (L2-L4).

---

## The 4-Layer Forensic Stack

### Layer 1: Content (What they say)

- **Least reliable.** Subject to censorship, performative focus.
- *Analysis*: Note keywords, stated goals.
- *Weight*: 10%.

### Layer 2: Frequency & Syntax (How they say it)

- **Harder to fake.**
- *Initiation Ratio*: Who starts? (Power dynamic indicator).
- *Message Volume*: Text walls vs. one-worders (Investment assymetry).
- *Latency*: Reply speed (Priority indicator).
- *Structure*: Formal vs. slang, emoji use (Emotional leakage).
- *Weight*: 30%.

### Layer 3: Interaction Dynamics (The Dance)

- **Power Flows.**
- *Question/Answer Ratio*: Who asks? Who answers? (Questioner holds power).
- *Frame Control*: Who sets the topic? Who follows?
- *Validation Loops*: Who seeks approval? Who grants it?
- *Weight*: 40%.

### Layer 4: Schema Projection (The Soul)

- **The "Why".**
- *Isomorphism*: Does this chat pattern mirror other life patterns? (e.g., Anxious in chat = Anxious in career).
- *Core Wounds*: What triggers defensiveness/withdrawal?
- *Shadow*: What is consistently avoided?
- *Weight*: 20% (The Synthesis).

---

## Diagnostic Indicators

| Indicator | Interpretation |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Double Texting (3+)** | Anxious attachment, fear of abandonment. |
| **Zero Questions** | Narcissism, lack of interest, or extreme "Receiver" frame. |
| **Explaining (JADE)** | Trauma response (Justify, Argue, Defend, Explain). Submissive frame. |
| **Topic Hopping** | ADHD or Avoidance of intimacy. |
| **Mirroring** | High empathy (fawn) or Manipulation (sociopathic). Check intent. |
| **"Hahaha" / "Lol"** | Anxiety buffer. Softener. Fear of conflict. |

---

## Protocol Execution

1. **Ingest**: Load raw chat logs (JSON/Text).
2. **Sanitize**: Remove PII, normalize timestamps.
3. **Metrics**: Calculate ratios (I:R, word count, latency).
4. **Profile**: Map L2/L3 patterns to L4 schemas.
5. **Verdict**: Construct "User Profile" and "Relationship Dynamic" report.
