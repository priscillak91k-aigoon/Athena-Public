# Protocol 053: Kinetic Information Engine (Compute over Storage)

> **Core Axiom**: "Storage is potential energy. Compute turn it into kinetic intelligence."
> **Mechanic**: Utilizing the raw dimensions of local data (Graph Structure, Text Statistics) to generate insights that are impossible via standard search APIs.

## 1. The Three Capabilities

### 🗺️ Capability A: Topical Authority Mapper (Category Graph)

* **Problem**: Keyword search is scattershot. You miss the "unknown unknowns."
* **Mechanism**: Traverse the Wikipedia Category Tree (Parent -> Children).
* **Output**: A hierarchical Taxonomy of 500+ sub-topics for total SEO dominance.
* **Tool**: `CategoryWalker` (Recursive Graph Traversal).

### 🧪 Capability B: The Jargon Injector (TF-IDF)

* **Problem**: AI copy sounds generic ("Look younger"). Expert copy uses precision vocabulary ("Restore malar fat pads").
* **Mechanism**: **TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency)**.
  * Compare the *Target Cluster* (e.g., "Dermatology") vs. the *General Corpus*.
  * Extract words that are high-frequency *locally* but low-frequency *globally*.
* **Output**: A "Gold List" of high-status signaling words to inject into context.
* **Tool**: `JargonInjector` (Scikit-Learn).

### 🔗 Capability C: The Serendipity Walker (Graph Traversal)

* **Problem**: Innovation requires connecting dissimilar concepts. Vector search optimizes for similarity (boring).
* **Mechanism**: Find the "Shortest Path" or "Random Walk" between two disparate nodes in the internal link graph.
* **Example**: `Tuition` -> `Recruitment` -> `Conscription` -> `Military Strategy`.
* **Output**: A logical "Bridge Concept" to transfer authority from one domain to another.
* **Tool**: `SerendipityPath` (NetworkX).
