About Color Formats

HEX codes (#RRGGBB) are the most common color format in web development. RGB (Red, Green, Blue) defines colors by their component intensities (0-255). HSL (Hue, Saturation, Lightness) is more intuitive for designers — hue is the color wheel angle, saturation is vibrancy, and lightness is brightness.
