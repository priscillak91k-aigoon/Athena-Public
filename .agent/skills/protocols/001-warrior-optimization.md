# Protocol 001: Warrior Optimization

> **Purpose**: A lifestyle and work optimization protocol tailored to the user's specific DNA profile (COMT G/G Warrior, CLOCK A/A Morning Lark).

## Rules of Engagement
1. **The 3-Hour Golden Window**: All complex analytical and high-stakes work MUST happen in the first 3 hours after waking up.
2. **Artificial Chaos**: If the environment is too slow/mundane, artificially inject stakes and tight deadlines to engage the rapid-dopamine metabolism (Warrior phenotype) to stay motivated.
3. **Hard Cutoff on Caffeine**: Zero caffeine after 12:00 PM due to slow CYP1A2 metabolism.
4. **Sleep as a Hard Boundary**: 8 hours is non-negotiable. Compromised BDNF means learning new complex tasks plummets during sleep deprivation. 
5. **Physical Output**: Prioritize high-weight resistance training over endurance to trigger rapid physiological transformations (ADRB2 G/G).
