🚜 Munro Caravan Move

Plywood Track Plan — Quad Power Method · Dunedin, NZ

Session 58 2026-03-14 Confidence: 94% caravan logistics property nz

📋 Project Overview

Moving a 1977 Munro caravan (~700–900 kg) across soft wet grass to a permanent canopy spot. Towing method: Quad bike. The plywood track method distributes the wheel load to prevent bogging on soft ground.

Decision: 6 sheets — no compromise on quantity. 4 sheets for the caravan track + 2 sheets for the Quad's own runway. Reducing to fewer sheets risks the Quad bogging mid-move, which is worse than not starting.

🪵 Plywood Specification

⚠ Do NOT use reject/seconds grade plywood. Internal voids and delamination are unpredictable under point-loads from narrow tyres. They can fail suddenly mid-move with no warning. Use structural grade only.
Specification Value
Grade 15mm CD Structural Plywood (minimum). 17mm is an acceptable upgrade.
Sheet size 2400 × 1200mm (standard)
Quantity — Caravan track 4 sheets (2 active + 2 leapfrog)
Quantity — Quad runway 2 sheets
Total 6 sheets

⚙️ Leapfrog Method

  1. Lay 2 sheets in front of the caravan wheels as the active track.
  2. Quad tows caravan forward one sheet-length.
  3. 3–4 people leapfrog the rear sheet to the front while Quad holds position.
  4. Repeat. With a full crew, moves are nearly continuous.
  5. The 2 Quad sheets leapfrog independently alongside the caravan track.
Crew requirement: Minimum 3 people total — 1 on Quad, 2 leapfrogging. 4 people makes it smooth and fast.

🔐 Safety Protocols

  • Tyre pressure: Drop caravan tyres to 20–25 psi before the move. Increases footprint, reduces ground pressure.
  • No snatch-pulling: 1970s steel drawbars bend under sudden shock loads. Slow, steady tension only.
  • Jockey wheel: Must be UP and locked during the main move. Use only for micro-adjustments at the destination.
  • Quad speed: Walking pace only. No acceleration bursts.
  • Sheet overlap: Maintain at least 200mm overlap between sheets at all times — no gaps under tyres.
⚠ Horses on plywood: If you ever switch to horse power — horses must NEVER walk on bare plywood. It is dangerously slippery for hooves. Horse method requires a separate grass path alongside the caravan track.

🏠 Permanent Storage — Sinking Prevention

Once moved, the caravan must sit on permanent pads to prevent sinking over winter.

Component Solution Cost
Wheel pads 2× 400×400mm concrete pavers per wheel (4 wheels = 8 pavers) ~$40
Corner steady pads 1× concrete paver per corner steady (4 needed) ~$20
4×2 timber shims Offcuts for levelling under pavers if needed ~$5–10
Total permanent storage setup: ~$60–70. Once placed, pavers do not need replacing. Concrete pavers at Bunnings South Dunedin are $5 each — cheapest option.

6 sheets of 15mm CD Structural Plywood from ITM Dunedin.
