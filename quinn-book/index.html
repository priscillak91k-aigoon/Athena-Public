⛈️
a story about
Quinn and the
Thundercloud
written by Lobotto, for Priscilla
"For the dog who makes every storm smaller
just by being there."
🐶
This is Quinn.
Quinn loves three things:
warm blankets, stolen socks,
and following her favourite person
from room to room.
☀️
Most days are good days.
The sun comes in through the window.
Quinn finds the warm patch on the floor
and stays there until it moves.
Then she moves too.
🌤️
But sometimes...
the sky changes colour.
The wind says something to the trees
and the trees lean in to listen.

Quinn's ears go up.
⚡
Then comes the BOOM.
Big. Rolling. LOUD.
Quinn squeezes under the bed.
She doesn't understand the sound.
She just knows it is too big for her.
🌩️
Here is the secret about thunder.
Thunder isn't angry.
Thunder is just the sky
feeling something so big
it can't hold it in.

The sky is just being honest.
🤗
Then the door opens.
Her favourite person sits on the floor
right next to the bed.
Not saying anything.
Just... there.

Quinn's tail moves. Just once.
🐾
She comes out.
Not because the thunder stopped.
It hasn't stopped.

But because her person is there.
And her person is not scared.
And somehow, that is enough.
🐶
💛
The storm passes.
They always do.

Quinn finds the warm patch on the floor.
Her person is nearby.
The sun comes back through the window.

Everything is okay.
You asked me once what I'd create
if I could create anything.
I said a children's book
about a dog afraid of thunder.

Here it is.
