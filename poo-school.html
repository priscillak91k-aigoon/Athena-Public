🍕 THE BIG JOURNEY!

From food to poo in 1 to 3 days! Follow the food and hit NEXT STOP!

Stop 1 of 8
👄

YOUR MOUTH

Food starts here! Your teeth chomp it into tiny pieces and saliva (spit!) starts to dissolve it. Your tongue pushes it to the back so you can swallow!

⏱️ Takes: About 30 seconds
🌀

OESOPHAGUS (The Slide!)

This is a tube from your mouth to your stomach. Food doesn't just FALL down — muscles SQUEEZE it along, like toothpaste in a tube! Even upside down!

⏱️ Takes: About 10 seconds
🫃

YOUR STOMACH

The stomach is a SQUISHING, CHURNING, ACID-FILLED bag! It mashes food into a thick gooey soup and kills most bad germs with acid strong enough to dissolve metal!

⏱️ Takes: 2–4 hours
🌿

SMALL INTESTINE (The Long Tunnel!)

This bendy tube is 6 METRES long — that's taller than a house! It absorbs all the good stuff — vitamins, sugar, fat, protein — into your blood to power your body!

⏱️ Takes: 2–6 hours
🌱

YOUR LIVER

The liver squirts BILE (a green liquid) into the intestine to help digest fat. Bile is what turns your poo its lovely brown colour! It's like the secret ingredient.

⏱️ Works while you eat!
🪱

LARGE INTESTINE (The Drying Machine!)

What's left is mostly water and stuff the body can't use. The large intestine SUCKS OUT the water (so your body doesn't lose it) and squeezes everything into the shape of... POO!

⏱️ Takes: 10–59 hours
💩

THE RECTUM (The Waiting Room!)

Poo hangs out here waiting for the right moment! Nerves tell your brain "IT'S TIME!" and that's when you feel the urge to go. Your body holds it until you find a toilet — mostly!

⏱️ Waits until you're ready!
🚽

THE TOILET! 🎉

FREEDOM! The muscles relax and out it comes! One pizza today becomes one poo in 1–3 days. Your body kept every useful bit and sent the rest here. AMAZING!

⏱️ Total journey: 1–3 DAYS!

🐾 AMAZING ANIMAL POOS!

Animals do some SERIOUSLY weird things with their poo...

Flipped: 0 / 8 🃏
🐨
KOALA
Babies eat something disgusting...
👆 FLIP ME!
🐨
Baby koalas eat their MUM'S POO! 😱 It gives them the special gut bacteria they need to digest eucalyptus leaves. Without the poo meal, they'd die!
🦘
WOMBAT
Their poo has a very unusual shape...
👆 FLIP ME!
🦘
Wombats are the ONLY animals that do SQUARE poos! 🟫 They make about 80–100 cube poos per night to mark their territory — cubes don't roll away!
🐦
BIRDS
Their white poo is actually TWO things in one...
👆 FLIP ME!
🐦
Birds poo and wee at the SAME TIME from the same hole! 🕳️ The white part is their wee (uric acid) and the dark bit is the poo. Maximum efficiency!
🐘
ELEPHANT
They produce an ENORMOUS amount...
👆 FLIP ME!
🐘
An elephant poos up to 150 KILOGRAMS every single day! 💩 That's heavier than two adult humans! People in Africa make PAPER from elephant poo — it's actually useful!
🐰
RABBIT
They eat something very surprising...
👆 FLIP ME!
🐰
Rabbits eat their OWN POO! 🍃 But only the soft night-poos called cecotropes — they're full of vitamins. The hard round poos they leave behind. If a rabbit can't reach to eat them, they get very sick.
🐍
SNAKE
They only need to poo very rarely...
👆 FLIP ME!
🐍
A snake might only poo ONCE EVERY FEW WEEKS or even months! 🗓️ They eat whole animals so digestion takes forever. A python that ate a deer once didn't poo for TWO MONTHS!
🌊
WHALE
Ocean scientists LOVE whale poo...
👆 FLIP ME!
🌊
Whale poo is BRIGHT ORANGE and floats on the water! 🟠 Scientists collect it to learn what whales eat. A blue whale's poo is the size of a CAR — and it actually helps plants grow in the ocean!
☕
CIVET CAT
People make COFFEE from this animal's poo...
👆 FLIP ME!
☕
The world's most expensive coffee — Kopi Luwak — is made from coffee beans that a civet cat has EATEN AND POOED OUT! ☕💩 People pay hundreds of dollars for a tiny cup. Some grown-ups drink poo coffee!
