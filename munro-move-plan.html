📋 Move-Day Execution Checklist

🌅 Pre-Move (Morning — aim for 7-8 AM)

  • Book delivery for morning of move day (ground is firmest before sun hits it)
  • Confirm 4 people available: 1 Quad driver, 1 caravan guide, 2 ply shufflers
  • Drop Munro tyre pressure to 20–25 psi (increases footprint, grips ply)
  • Raise jockey wheel fully up and locked
  • Pick up concrete pavers from Bunnings (can do day before)

� The Move

  1. Lay Track: Place 2 caravan sheets end-to-end under wheels (4.8m runway). Place 1 Quad sheet alongside.
  2. Begin: Quad tows Munro forward onto the runway. Caravan guide walks alongside.
  3. Leapfrog (Caravan): As the Munro rolls forward, ply shufflers grab the rear caravan sheet → run it to the front → lay it down.
  4. Leapfrog (Quad): Same technique — grab rear Quad sheet → place ahead of Quad.
  5. Repeat until the Munro reaches the canopy spot.
  6. If Quad spins on ply → STOP. Reposition. Don't force it.
⚠️ DO NOT snatch-pull the Munro with a 4WD — 1970s steel drawbar will bend.

🏁 Final Positioning

  1. Once Munro is within ~1m of final spot, lower jockey wheel.
  2. Disconnect from Quad. Use jockey wheel for micro-adjustments by hand.
  3. Place concrete pavers under each tyre (×2) and each corner steady (×4).
  4. Place 4×2 timber + ply scrap under jockey wheel as permanent pad.
  5. Re-inflate tyres to normal pressure.
  6. Deploy corner steadies onto pavers. Level the caravan.
  7. Chock wheels.

🧹 Post-Move

  • Stack plywood sheets and lean against fence — reusable for future projects
  • Check nothing sunk or shifted after 24 hours
