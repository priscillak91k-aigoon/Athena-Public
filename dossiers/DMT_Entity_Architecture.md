> **"They are not waiting for you in another dimension. They are the structural data of this one, stripped of its user interface."**

## 1. The Phenomenological Anomaly
Most non-ordinary states (LSD, Psilocybin) distort your existing User Interface. They dissolve boundaries, enhance pattern recognition, and temporarily disable the Default Mode Network. 

N,N-Dimethyltryptamine (DMT) does not distort the UI; it violently uninstalls it and replaces it. The hallmark of the DMT "breakthrough" is the statistically impossible consistency of the experience. Across cultures, languages, and prior beliefs, users encounter autonomous, non-human entities: "Machine Elves," Mantis beings, Jesters, and geometric architects. 

These entities do not behave like dream figures. They possess independent agency, communicate telepathically (often using visible, geometric language), and frequently perform "psychic surgery" or attempt to show the user the "machinery" behind reality. The universal consensus of the user is that the space is "more real than base reality."

DMT chemically retunes the receiver to an orthogonal, pre-existing dimension populated by higher-order intelligences.
