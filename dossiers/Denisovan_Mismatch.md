# DOSSIER: The Evolutionary Mismatch (Paleolithic Survival Architecture)

> **"You are not broken; you are miscalibrated for the 21st century."**

## 1. The Core Thesis
Modern clinical psychology pathologizes what evolutionary biology spent millions of years optimizing. The specific intersection of your genetic markers—**COMT G/G**, **OXTR A/A**, and **TNF-Alpha A/G**—layered beneath a high-masking AuDHD phenotype, is not a "disorder." It is a highly specialized, paleolithic software package designed for extreme survival in low-density, high-threat environments (e.g., Ice Age Eurasia, correlating heavily with Neanderthal/Denisovan introgression). 

When you run this software on modern, low-threat, civilian hardware, the system idles too low, hallucinates threats, and turns its defensive mechanisms inward.

## 2. The Genetic Architecture (The Sentinel Phenotype)

### COMT G/G (The Executioner)
* **Mechanic**: Rapid dopamine clearance.
* **Evolutionary Purpose**: In a relaxed state, the COMT Warrior brain is bored, unmotivated, and seeking novelty. But under extreme stress, when others' brains are flooded with neurotoxic levels of dopamine causing panic and paralysis, the G/G brain clears the excess. The result is lethal, cold clarity in the middle of chaos. 
* **The Mismatch**: In 2026, you don't face apex predators. You face spreadsheets and social obligations. The brain creates anxiety to simulate the high-stakes environment it needs to wake up.

### OXTR A/A (The Observer)
* **Mechanic**: Lower social reward sensitivity. Analytical processing of social bonding.
* **Evolutionary Purpose**: Prevents the individual from being emotionally compromised by the "herd." If the tribe panics, the A/A individual does not get drunk on the collective hysteria. You remain the detached observer, making calculated decisions to ensure survival rather than prioritizing social cohesion.
* **The Mismatch**: You process social interactions mechanically rather than chemically. This requires massive computational overhead (masking), leading to severe post-social exhaustion.

### TNF-Alpha A/G (The Biological Shield)
* **Mechanic**: Hyper-reactive inflammatory response.
* **Evolutionary Purpose**: Designed to violently attack paleolithic pathogens from raw meat, contaminated water, or untreated physical trauma. 
* **The Mismatch**: Because it lacks a genuine external enemy (like a saber-toothed tiger bite or a lethal parasite), the immune system idles high and eventually attacks localized tissue (e.g., the pelvic cysts). The physical inflammation is the biological mirror to your psychological hyper-vigilance.

## 3. The AuDHD Component (Threat Scanning)
Sensory processing "disorders" are a modern reframing of **hyper-vigilant environmental scanning**. 
* Hearing the electrical hum of a refrigerator is a bug today; hearing the faint snap of a twig at 50 yards was a feature. 
* "Hyper-focus" is the ability to lock onto a singular survival task (tracking, tool-making) until completion, bypassing the need for immediate biological rewards. 

## 4. Non-Ordinary States (The Reset Switch)
This rigid, hyper-vigilant Default Mode Network (DMN) requires massive metabolic energy to maintain. 
* **The Intervention**: Tools like LSD (Johns Hopkins protocol) or Holotropic Breathwork bypass the COMT G/G bottleneck. 
* **The Mechanic**: By forcibly dissolving the DMN, you stop the environmental scanning. This signals the central nervous system that the "war is over," allowing the TNF-Alpha inflammatory cascade to finally stand down. 

## 5. Synthesis
You are running military-grade survival software in a civilian environment. The goal is not to "fix" the software, but to understand its mechanics, manually trigger its reset switches (via breathwork, psychedelics, or deep novelty), and stop treating its defensive adaptations as personal failures.
