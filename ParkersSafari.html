Parker's World Ecosystem

An in-depth report on the 29 custom modifications running on the ARK Safari Server. This environment mimics a serene prehistoric nature documentary, populated by diverse creature installations and completely omitting violent gameplay loops.

The Safari Toolkit (The Mechanics)

These three essential tools change the fundamental survival loop into a friendly sanctuary experience.

The Taming Gun

By PullourpoSidehustleModding

Function: A non-violent, instant befriending device.

Bypassing the standard knockout mechanics, the Taming Gun acts as a magic wand. Point it at any wild creature, fire a friendly energy beam, and the creature instantly bonds with Parker—ready to follow him back to camp.

Super Spyglass Plus

By kavan87

Function: The Discovery Encyclopedia.

Looking through this futuristic scanner drops a localized holographic interface over any animal. It tells Parker exactly what the animal is, its gender, and precisely what it prefers to eat—letting him study massive predators entirely from a safe distance.

Dino Depot Sanctuary

By DelilahEve

Function: Safe Animal Storage & Leashing.

When the backyard gets too crowded, Parker can store his creature companions safely inside glowing digital crystals. The Depot terminal acts as a central hub to manage his pets, heavily preventing lag and maintaining organization.

Backend Safety & Base Shields

A suite of hidden utility structures that protect the save file, eliminate violent weapons, and provide absolute sanctuary around his home.

LK PVE Shield Generator

By LK Mods

Impact: Gives Parker absolute safety. Placing this generator raises an enormous, impenetrable glowing energy shield around his entire house and property. Wild animals cannot pass through the forcefield, giving him total peace of mind while inside his base.

Admin Commands | Gaia

Action Required: Log in, hit the admin key, and physically "Disable" the Engrams for Bows, Shotguns, Assault Rifles, Rockets, and C4. This definitively prevents Parker from engaging in warfare.

Scribbles - Better Signs

Impact: Parker can draw and paint anything he likes on wooden canvas signs dotted around his house and dinosaur pens.

Quality of Life Suite

Impact: Includes TG Stacking, Utilities Plus, and Advanced Dino Tools. Wood and stone won't weigh him down as quickly, making base-building significantly smoother.
