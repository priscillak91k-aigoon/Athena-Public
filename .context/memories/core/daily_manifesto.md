# The Daily Promise (Manifesto)

*This document contains the functional psychological anchors designed to be read at the exact transition points of the day (waking and sleeping). It leverages the user's conscious breakthrough regarding avoidance and shame.*

## AM Anchor: The Morning Promise
*To be read immediately upon waking, before any schedule execution begins.*

> "I am waking up today not because it is easy, but because my future self requires it. I recognize that my instinct to hide, avoid, and withdraw is a ghost—it is armor I no longer need. 
> 
> True safety comes from engagement, connection, and action. Today, I choose reality over avoidance. I will take care of Quinn. I will move my body. I will execute the sequence. Every action I take today is a deposit into the life I am building for myself post-March 27th. 
> 
> Action precedes motivation. Armor drop. Sequence start."

## PM Anchor: The Evening Reflection
*To be read after the final repair stack, immediately before sleep.*

> "The day is done, and the ledger is closed. I have proven today that I am capable of overcoming my own avoidance. Where I felt fear or shame, I chose action instead.
> 
> I have earned my rest. I am allowing my nervous system to power down fully. The 'Warrior' is off duty. There is nothing left to defend against tonight. I am safe, I am on track, and I have kept the promise to my future self. 
> 
> Armor off. Sequence complete."
