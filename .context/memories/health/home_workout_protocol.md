# Home Workout: Longevity & Joint Armor

> **Goal**: Maintain muscle mass, bulletproof joints to prevent aging injuries, and keep systemic inflammation low (CRP/Ferritin aware). 
> **Equipment**: 10kg DB, 3kg DBs, 8kg Kettlebell (KB), Adjustable Bench.

## 🏆 The "Centenarian Decathlon" Philosophy (Dr. Peter Attia / Outlive)
*We are not training to be a bodybuilder. We are training specifically so that at 90-100 years old, you can:*
1. Get up off the floor with a single point of support.
2. Pick up a 30lb (15kg) grandchild or dog.
3. Carry groceries up the stairs without being winded. 

**Attia's 4 Pillars applied to this routine:**
1. **Stability (The Cornerstone):** Your Plank to Bear crawls, Deadbugs, and Kettlebell Halos represent DNS (Dynamic Neuromuscular Stabilization). They teach your body to decelerate force safely.
2. **Strength (Muscle = Survival):** The 3-day split below (Workout A, B, C) fulfills Attia's baseline 2-3x weekly hypertrophy rule.
3. **Zone 2 Cardio (Mitochondrial Engine):** Your daily 1-hour walk with Quinny covers your 3-4 hours/week of Aerobic Base training. Keep a "conversational but strained" brisk pace.
4. **VO2 Max (Peak Output):** Due to your high CRP (inflammation), pushing into true Zone 5/Failure is currently restricted. Instead, we use **Time Under Tension (Tempo)**. Slow down the movement to make light weights feel heavier without spiking cortisol.

## ⚖️ The "Left Side First" Rule (Imbalance Protocol)
Since your left side is weaker:
1. **Always start with your left arm/leg** on unilateral exercises (like split squats or single-arm rows).
2. Count how many reps the left side can do with good form.
3. **Only do that same number of reps on your right side**, even if the right side could do 5 more. This forces the left side to catch up.

## 🦶 Foot & Calf Rehab (Plantar Fasciitis / Sprain Prevention)
*Add this 3-minute circuit to the end of every workout or your 1-hour dog walk.*
1. **Kneeling Heel Sit (Plantar Stretch):** Kneel on the floor, tuck your toes under, and sit back on your heels. Hold for 30 seconds.
2. **Slow Calf Raises (Eccentric Focus):** Stand on a step. Rise up on both toes, lift the right foot off the step, and slowly lower yourself down using *only* your left calf. Repeat 10x per leg.
3. **Frozen Water Bottle Roll:** Keep a frozen water bottle in the freezer. Roll your left arch over it for 2 minutes after long walks.

## 🕒 The Strategy: Time Under Tension (Tempo)
Since you are limited to 10kg/8kg max, you will use **Tempo Training**:
* **3-1-1 Tempo**: 3 seconds lowering the weight, 1 second pause at the bottom, 1 second to lift it back up.

## Workout A: Lower Body & Core (The Foundation)
*Focus: Glutes, hamstrings, and core stability (protects the lower back).*

1. **Goblet Squats** (8kg KB or 10kg DB)
   * *Hold weight at chest. Sit back onto the bench, then stand up.*
   * 3 sets of 10-12 reps (Slow 3-second descent).
2. **Romanian Deadlifts (RDLs)** (Hold 10kg DB in one hand, 8kg KB in the other)
   * *Hinge at the hips, slight knee bend. Feel the stretch in hamstrings.*
   * 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
3. **Bulgarian Split Squats** (Bodyweight to start, then add 3kg DBs)
   * *One foot resting back on the bench. Drop back knee straight down.*
   * 3 sets of 8 reps per leg. (Incredible for knee health and balance).
4. **Plank to Bear Crawl Hold**
   * *Hold a plank for 30 seconds, then drop knees 1 inch off the floor (bear hold) for 20 seconds.*
   * 3 sets.

---

## Workout B: Upper Body Pull & Shoulders (Posture Correction)
*Focus: Reversing rounded shoulders and strengthening the upper back.*

1. **Single-Arm Dumbbell Row** (10kg DB)
   * *One hand and knee on the flat bench. Pull elbow to hip.*
   * 3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm.
2. **Kettlebell Halo** (8kg KB)
   * *Hold KB upside down by the horns. Circle it slowly around your head.*
   * 3 sets of 10 rotations (5 each direction). (Amazing for shoulder mobility).
3. **Seated Overhead Press** (2x 3kg DBs)
   * *Sit on bench (back adjusted straight up). Press weights overhead.*
   * 3 sets of 15 reps (Go very slow on the way down).
4. **Bench Reverse Flyes** (2x 3kg DBs)
   * *Lie chest-down on an incline bench. Lift arms out like wings.*
   * 3 sets of 15 reps.

---

## Workout C: Full Body Functional
*Focus: Moving through multiple planes of motion to keep joints lubricated.*

1. **Kettlebell Deadbug** (8kg KB)
   * *Lie on back, hold KB straight up over chest. Extend opposite arm and leg to the floor without letting lower back arch.*
   * 3 sets of 10 reps per side. (Elite core movement).
2. **Incline Bench Push-ups**
   * *Hands on the elevated bench to reduce load on shoulders.*
   * 3 sets to 2 reps shy of failure.
3. **Goblet Reverse Lunges** (8kg KB)
   * *Hold KB at chest. Step backward into a lunge.*
   * 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.
4. **Farmers Carry** (10kg DB in one hand, 8kg KB in the other)
   * *Stand tall, shoulders back. Walk holding the weights for 45-60 seconds. Switch hands and repeat.*
   * 3 sets. (Incredible for grip strength, posture, and core).
